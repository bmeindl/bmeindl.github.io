Building products that learn so students can, too.

I turn AI from buzzword into product. At IU Internationale Hochschule, I manage SynteaOS, an agentic learning platform that personalises study paths for 120,000+ distance-education students. With roots in management consulting and a PhD in Industry 4.0 analytics, I bridge the gap between strategy and execution.

Off-duty, you'll find me on a yoga mat or chasing waves in Portugal. I'm passionate about mindfulness and late-night debates on whether and how large language models can meditate.

AI-Based Role Plays for Medical Education

Teaching anamnesis interviews through virtual patient simulations

This study presents a novel approach to educational role-playing through an AI-based bot, leveraging GPT-4 to simulate anamnesis interviews in healthcare education. Students from different health study programs engage in complex patient-health professional conversations, with results showing high satisfaction and realism ratings.

The Fourth Industrial Revolution and Jobs

Technology impact on occupations and labor markets

Research project to describe the relation of technologies of the fourth industrial evolution and jobs. We use natural language processing to link patents to task descriptions and create 4IR exposure scores at an occupation and task level.

September 12, 2019

Binary Patent Classification Methods

For economic analysis with few labeled samples

We develop binary patent classification algorithms for ambiguous concepts and small sample sizes. Our analysis compares naive Bayes, SVM, random forest, and KNN classifiers with a CNN model, showing highest accuracy for pre-trained CNN models, especially beneficial for small sample sizes.
